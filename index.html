About Me

Hi! My name is Abbie, and I'm a third year student at The University of Windsor. I'm currently studying Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering.

My interest in programming started back in when I was 12. My brother joined the local FIRST robotics team, and I was instantly hooked. From that day forward, I spent any moment I could learning how to program robots until I was old enough to join the team myself.

Since graduating from the team in 2020, I have been mentoring FRC team 772, and volunteering at many FIRST events! I have been a referee for 9 FLL qualifing and provincial events and more recently a CSA (Control System Advisor) for 3 FRC qualifying and provincial events!
